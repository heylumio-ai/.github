# Lumio

**AI-ready commerce for ecommerce merchants.**

Lumio helps ecommerce stores show up when shoppers ask AI — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Google AI Overviews — for product recommendations. We score catalogs for AI readiness, enrich product data at the SKU level, and activate that enriched data across the channels that matter: Google Merchant Center feeds, Schema.org JSON-LD, and platform-native metafields.

[heylumio.ai](https://heylumio.ai)

---

### What we do

Shoppers increasingly start product research inside AI assistants. Those assistants recommend products based on what they can read and trust about a catalog — not on clever keywords. Lumio turns ecommerce catalogs into the kind of structured, rich, trustworthy data that AI systems surface.

- **AI readiness scoring** — per-SKU scores across six dimensions, so you know exactly where catalog gaps hurt discoverability.
- **Content enrichment** — targeted improvements to titles, descriptions, attributes, and structured data.
- **Activation** — enriched data delivered where it counts: GMC supplemental feeds, JSON-LD on product pages, and platform metafields.
- **Prompt-level benchmarking** — track how your products show up across AI platforms, and how you compare to the brands appearing alongside you.

### Who we build for

Mid-market ecommerce merchants — typically $1M–$50M GMV — who take product content seriously and want to be ready for how shoppers actually find things now.

### How we work

Small team. Product-led. Shipping quickly and iterating in public where we can. We use AI heavily in our own stack — both to build Lumio and to run the company.
